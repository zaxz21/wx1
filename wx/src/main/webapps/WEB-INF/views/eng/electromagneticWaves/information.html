What is microwave?

Let the frequency of single frequency electromagnetic wave be f and the period be t. the propagation speed of electromagnetic wave in vacuum is equal to the speed of light C = 3 × 10 ^ 8 m / s, then the corresponding wavelength in free space is λ= cT=c/f （0 – 1 – 1） Generally, the electromagnetic wave with frequency of 300MHz ~ 3000ghz is called microwave. According to formula (0 – 1 – 1), the corresponding free space wavelength is 1m ~ 0.1mm. Microwave has high frequency and short wavelength, so it is called microwave. The "micro" of microwave is in terms of its wavelength. On the other hand, "micro" of microwave is a relative concept, which mainly refers to that the wavelength of microwave is "micro" relative to the size of human body, conventional circuit and general obstacles in nature. Microwave is also an electromagnetic wave, so the knowledge about electromagnetic wave learned in the course of "electromagnetic field theory" is also applicable to microwave. "Microwave technology" course will continue to learn about electromagnetic waves on the basis of "electromagnetic field theory". The microwave frequency band is between meter wave (also known as ultrashort wave or VHF wave) and far-infrared wave.
