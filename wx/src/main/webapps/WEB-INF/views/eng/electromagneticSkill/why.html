Why should we learn?

Why study microwave? What's the use of learning microwave technology? This is usually a concern for beginners. Most practical wireless communication systems work in the microwave band, and their information transmission must be inseparable from the "wire", and one of the important contents of the microwave technology course is to learn all kinds of "wires" suitable for the microwave band. The above problems are the basic problems of information transmission in the communication system, and are the basic knowledge that all practitioners of electronic information and communication engineering should be familiar with and master.
