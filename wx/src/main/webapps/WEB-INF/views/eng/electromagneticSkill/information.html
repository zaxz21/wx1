What is microwave technology?

Microwave technology has a wide range, and its meaning can be divided into broad sense and narrow sense. Microwave technology in a broad sense can include microwave generation, transmission, transformation (including amplification and modulation), transmission, reception, measurement and so on. Microwave technology in a narrow sense mainly studies the problems of microwave transmission, including some commonly used microwave transmission lines and microwave passive components. These contents are generally described in undergraduate microwave technology courses of electronic information engineering, communication engineering, electromagnetic field and wireless technology, and are the basis for studying other microwave technology problems.
